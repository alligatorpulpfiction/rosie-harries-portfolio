About Rosie

Rosie Harries has spent her career producing live arts performances, painting, tattooing and managing an arts charity based in East London. She is now turning her mind to coding, and is ready for the next adventure! She is looking to work with arts organisations and charities to engage new audiences through developing their websites & online presences.
